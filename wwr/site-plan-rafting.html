Overview

Purpose

Its purpose is to achieve communication, team coordination and motivation to surpass new goals set. It also promotes a good lifestyle to keep ahead, thus achieving great results.

Audience

The whole world has a great variety of rivers located in coastal, mountainous and jungle regions. The force of the water in many of these rivers allows rafting to be practiced, making this sport to be considered pure adrenaline. The rivers are classified according to their difficulty, on an international scale from I to VI. On the other hand, this sport uses inflatable boats or blasas, which are propelled by oars and steered from the stern, carrying the crew and the raft through the turbulent waters of the rivers.
